about

I, Ifeanyi Iheme Michael, am a Frontend-Web developer living in Lagos, Nigeria. I strives to build beautiful web applications through quality and sustainable code and user-centric design.

Since graduating with B.A in Creative Arts from Obafemi Awolowo University and as one who is open and committed to learning new things, i have focused my professional career almost exclusively on front-end development. My experience in frontend development has helped me developed a strong problem solving skill. I have participated in several collaborative and personal projects and i'm also participative in the open source community.

I have earned serval Professional certifications in Web development from Google, Meta and the university of Michigan. I have participated in an internship and hackathon. I am a team player and love the community of developers. I engage in non-coding activities such as reading, creating and arranging music, playing musical instruments and travelling. I am eager to implement my skills and abilities productively in whatever capacity provided.
